Ouch!

May 2020

Ouch! My cousin blurted
When I threw myself into her embrace.
Ouch! My friend shouted
When I was patting his face.
Ouch! My teacher whispered
When I high-fived with him for the race.

Ouch! My heart hurt
When I was expelled
For attacks? Assaults? Aggressiveness?

Ouch! When I was born,
When the world made me hyperactive,
When I was misunderstood...
Ouch! That hurts.
